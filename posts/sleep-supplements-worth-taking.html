01

Magnesium L-Threonate (Magtein)

Minerals
FormL-Threonate
Dose144mg elemental
Timing1–2 hrs before bed

L-Threonate is the only form of magnesium shown to cross the blood-brain barrier in meaningful quantities — which is relevant specifically for its effects on sleep architecture and cognitive function rather than the broader enzymatic roles other magnesium forms fill. Research by MIT suggests it raises brain magnesium levels where other forms don't. It's significantly more expensive than glycinate, so only worth considering if you're already taking magnesium for other functions and want a sleep-specific form.

Worth it because
  • Only magnesium form shown to cross blood-brain barrier
  • MIT-developed with published research behind it
  • Specific mechanism relevant to sleep quality
The catch
  • Significantly more expensive than standard magnesium
  • Requires 3–4 capsules per dose to hit effective amount

02

Ashwagandha KSM-66 Extract 600mg

Adaptogens
ExtractKSM-66 (root only)
Dose600mg daily
Withanolides≥5%

KSM-66 is the most clinically studied ashwagandha extract — root-only, with over 24 human clinical trials behind it. The mechanism for sleep is indirect: it reduces cortisol, which is often elevated in people with poor sleep. The effect isn't sedative but rather stress-modulating, which means it's more useful for people whose poor sleep is driven by elevated stress than for general insomnia. Extract quality varies enormously — KSM-66 branded extract is the benchmark to look for.

Worth it because
  • KSM-66 is the most researched ashwagandha extract
  • Consistent evidence for stress and cortisol reduction
  • Root-only extract — more standardised than whole plant
The catch
  • Less useful if poor sleep isn't stress-related
  • Takes 4–8 weeks of consistent use for full effect

03

L-Theanine 200mg

Amino Acids
Dose200mg
SourceGreen tea amino acid
Timing30–60 mins before bed

L-Theanine is the amino acid responsible for the calm, focused state that tea produces despite containing caffeine. At 200mg it promotes alpha brain wave activity — associated with relaxed alertness — without sedation. It doesn't knock you out but reduces the mental chatter that keeps many people awake. One of the few supplements where the effect is noticeable on the first use rather than after weeks of loading. Well-tolerated, inexpensive, and genuinely useful for sleep onset difficulty.

Worth it because
  • Effect noticeable from first use — no loading phase
  • Non-sedating — promotes calm without grogginess
  • Very inexpensive per dose
The catch
  • Addresses sleep onset only — not sleep quality throughout the night
  • Effect is subtle for some people
